Introduction of some fun instruments

I will introduce four fun instruments. They are piano, violin, tabla, and clarinet. Click here to see the picture of my favorite instrument!
Piano belongs to keyboard family, which means we can play it with our hands like typing on keyboard.

Violin is a string instrument. From its appearance, we can clearly see strings on it. Then, we just need to use the bow and rosin to make sound.

I do not know much about tabla, but I assume we can just hit its surface. It is pretty easy! Everyone can be a drum master!

To make sound on clarinet, we should first put the reed and blow, which will allow the reed to vibrate. The vibration of the reed and then the air create the sound.
